The Problem

Why your Czech isn't working

You've been doing apps for months. You can say "The apple is red" in Czech. Cool. Has that helped you once?

Apps teach you to talk like a parrot

You need: "Another beer, please" without sounding like a lost tourist.

Textbooks teach you tourist Czech

Formal, stiff, awkward. Real Czechs hear it and quietly cringe. Nobody says "Rád bych si objednal pivo" when they mean "Jedno pivo."

😐

Nobody explains the cultural layer

You said the right words but got a weird look. Why? Because Czech isn't just language — it's timing, tone, and unspoken rules. We cover all of it.
